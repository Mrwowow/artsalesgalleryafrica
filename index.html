About Art Sales Gallery Africa

We are a premier marketplace dedicated to connecting African artists with collectors and art enthusiasts worldwide, showcasing the rich diversity and exceptional quality of contemporary African art.

Founded in 2020, Art Sales Gallery Africa is committed to promoting the work of both established and emerging artists across the continent. Our carefully curated collection features fine art photography and mixed media pieces that reflect the vibrant culture, heritage, and creative spirit of Africa.

We believe in fair representation and transparent practices, ensuring that artists receive proper recognition and compensation for their work. By providing a platform for African artists to showcase their talent, we aim to bridge cultural divides and foster a deeper appreciation for African artistic expressions.

Artistic Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of artistic quality and authenticity in every piece we feature.

Fair Partnership

We maintain equitable relationships with our artists, ensuring they receive fair compensation and representation.

Cultural Celebration

We honor and promote the diverse cultural heritage and contemporary expressions of African art.
